To fix the height of a particular box in pixels, use .bfg__box--fixed and give the box the expected height.
In this case, one of the other boxes should fill the remaining space by having .bfg__box without any size (like .bfg__box--6).
To see it in action, open on a separate tab and resize the window. Note for IE11:
The .bfg__box--4 height is NOT accurate. To support this browser, only use 2 boxes in this context: one .bfg__box.bfg__box--fixed and one .bfg__box.
